Definition: Matrix Space

Let \(m, n \in \mathbb{Z}_+\) and let \(S\) be a set. The \(m \times n\) matrix space over \(S\) is the set of all \(m \times n\) matrices over \(S\), denoted \(\mathcal{M}_S(m, n)\). So, $$\mathcal{M}_S(m, n) = S^{\left[{1 \,.\,.\, m}\right] \times \left[{1 \,.\,.\, n}\right]}.$$
